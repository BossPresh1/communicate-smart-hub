🌍 Communicate Smart Hub

📘 What is Communication?

Communication is the process of sending and receiving messages between people.

👤 Sender → 💬 Message → 👂 Receiver → 🔁 Feedback

🗣 Types of Communication

🗨 Verbal – speaking

🤐 Non-verbal – body language

✍ Written – messages and emails

🎭 Formal vs Informal

Formal: “Good afternoon, I would like to present my idea.”

Informal: “Hey, let me show you something!”

💛 Non-Violent Communication

Speak calmly, avoid harsh words, and respect others’ feelings.

Instead of: “You are wrong!”

Say: “I see it differently, let me explain.”

🧠 Understand People

Listen carefully, observe body language, and be patient.

✨ Speak Clearly & Confidently

✔ Speak slowly

✔ Maintain eye contact

✔ Practice before speaking

✔ Believe in yourself

⚠ Do’s and Don’ts

✔ Be respectful

✔ Listen actively

✔ Be clear

✘ Don’t interrupt

✘ Don’t be rude

🌟 Why Communication Matters

Good communication builds relationships, prevents conflict, and helps you succeed in life.
