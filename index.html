📖 Communicate Smart

📘 What is Communication?

Communication is the process of sharing ideas, thoughts, and feelings between people.

🎭 Formal vs Informal Communication

Formal: Used in professional settings

Informal: Used with friends

💛 Non-Violent Communication

Speak with respect and calmness.

❌ “You are wrong!”

✔ “I see it differently, let me explain.”

🧠 Speak Confidently

❌ Head down, shy voice

✔ Look up, speak clearly

✨ Clear Communication Tips

  • Speak slowly
  • Maintain eye contact
  • Listen carefully
  • Be confident

🌟 Why Communication Matters

It helps build relationships, avoid misunderstandings, and succeed in life.
